Seal

Seals are 30-40% fat!

  • Size: 120 to 370 lbs
  • Max Diving Depth: 2,000 ft.
  • Lifespan: 25-30 years
  • Habitat: The ocean.

They have streamlined bodies and four limbs that are modified into flippers. Though not as fast in the water as dolphins, seals are more flexible and agile. Otariids use their front limbs primarily to propel themselves through the water, while phocids and walruses use their hind limbs. Otariids and walruses have hind limbs that can be pulled under the body and used as legs on land. By comparison, terrestrial locomotion by phocids is more cumbersome.
